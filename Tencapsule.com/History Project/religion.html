Religion

Clarenton has various Roman Catholic parishes for different areas within the town. These are overseen by a single cathedral for the entire town. The Roman Catholic religion is the official religion of Clarenton. As is ordained by the pope, the head of the church, please participate in necessary sacraments. Holidays are ordained by the church for us in Clarenton to celebrate.
